ABOUT

Generation Fest 2022 is a multi-cultural financial literacy and entertainment summit aimed to inspire people of all ages and nationalities. Our mission is to educate the community on the importance of becoming financial responsible through financial literacy panels and private 1-on-1 meeting sessions with the most knowledgeable financial experts in the world, brought to you by the most influential leaders and A-list entertains of today. Our job is to guide the next generation to a path of prosperity and righteousness. Welcome to Generation Fest 2022!
